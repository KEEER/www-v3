KEEER初创于2015年，由Queue Huo和Tiger Fan两位成员共同创立。后经Edgaru089, Alan Liang和Sunny Peng三名成员加入，队伍逐渐成型。本着要制作优秀游戏的初心，我们创建了客页工作室。

作为几个学生，我们的精力与时间也许有限，但我们将用内心中无限的开发热情与产品设计和技术相结合，做出令人满意、具有价值与意义的产品。

KEEER: a brief introduction

Together we, Queue Huo and Tiger Fan, founded KEEER in 2015. The group gradually formed as Alan Liang, Edgar Su and Sunny Peng joined in. We started this group first to make high quality games.

Now, we decide to make something more than just games; Now, we decide to start a new journey of developing; Now, we decide to enter a new world. We've reached an agreement to make educational applications, services, and even hardware gadgets. Every member in KEEER has infinite passion to innovate and develop.

As students, our time for developing is limited. However, the passion, the ideas and the technology we have will spur us to make practical and creative products.

Sincerely we thank everyone who supports KEEER. KEEER is now waiting for you, a skillful and a nice student developer.
